# Bill of Materials

| Qty | Part | Notes |
|-----|------|-------|
| 1 | ESP32 dev board | e.g. ESP32 DevKit V1 (any dev board with a free GPIO + 5V/GND works) |
| 1 | WS2812 / WS2812B LED strip | 13 LEDs at the strip's native pitch, or trim/extend and update `NUM_LEDS` in firmware config |
| 1 | USB-A → Micro-USB or USB-C cable, short | Powers the ESP32 from the printer's rear USB port |
| 4 | M3 heat-set inserts | Optional — for a removable/serviceable mount |
| 4 | M3x6 screws | Optional, pairs with the heat-set inserts |
| — | Double-sided tape | Alternative to heat-set inserts for mounting |

See [README.md](README.md) for wiring and assembly.
